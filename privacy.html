Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 Aug 2025

1) Scope

This policy applies to the PinFlicker website and the PinFlicker application that integrates with Pinterest via the official API.

2) Pinterest App Integration

When you connect your Pinterest account to PinFlicker, you grant access via Pinterest’s OAuth screen. We use this access only to perform the actions you approve (e.g., read your boards, create and publish pins).

  • We do not collect or store your Pinterest password.
  • We may temporarily process data such as account ID/username, board IDs, pin IDs/titles, images, and basic analytics needed to complete your request.
  • You can revoke access anytime in your Pinterest account settings.

3) Information we collect

  • Usage & log data: IP address, browser info, timestamps, and error logs used for security and debugging.
  • Content you provide: Pin titles, descriptions, and images you choose to upload.
  • Cookies (website only): Used for basic functionality; you can control cookies in your browser settings.

4) How we use information

  • To create/manage pins and boards you select
  • To provide support and improve app reliability
  • To prevent abuse and maintain security

5) Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only as necessary to operate the service (e.g., with Pinterest’s API) or to comply with legal obligations.

6) Security & retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

7) Your choices

  • Disconnect the app from Pinterest at any time in your Pinterest settings.
  • Request access or deletion by emailing admin@petsbunch.com.

8) Children

PinFlicker is not directed to children under 13.

9) Changes

We may update this policy and will post the new date at the top of this page.

10) Contact

Email: admin@petsbunch.com
